How we work together

No stickiness. No strings.
Just support.

Melita was built from the ground up to support independent HR consultants not compete with them.

  • No long-term contracts. Clients can disengage with 60 days' notice no penalties, no pressure, no exceptions.

  • No tech lock-ins. Clients sign directly with vendors. They own their systems not Melita. (ADP, Paylocity, Rippling, Paycom, and others.)

  • We train your client's replacements. When clients scale back services, we support the transition gracefully never resist it.

  • Fully modular. Clients engage only the services they need and can scale up or down at any time no bundling required.

  • Transparent pricing. PEPM model with 100% of broker commissions credited back to the client no hidden incentives.

  • Your brand, your call. Co-brand with Melita, keep the relationship entirely behind the scenes, or anything in between. Melita's team can communicate as an extension of your consultancy or operate independently as a strategic partner. Always your choice.

You define the relationship, stay in control, and we fill the gaps making you more impactful without ever replacing the work you do best.
